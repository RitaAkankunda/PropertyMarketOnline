## Method 3: Check ICANN Lookup (More Detailed)

1. **Go to:** https://lookup.icann.org
2. **Enter:** `propertymarketonline.com`
3. **Click "Lookup"**
4. **Scroll down** to find:
   - **"Registrar"** section
   - **"Registrar WHOIS Server"**
   - This will tell you the actual registrar
